Hi, I'm Ale!
I'm a mexican multidisciplinary designer and educator from Tijuana who believes the best ideas form at the intersection of disciplines—where architecture meets storytelling, and digital tools shake hands with handmade processes. My journey started in architecture and took me everywhere: directing creative projects for the Biennale Sessions project at the Venice Biennale of Architecture (2021), designing visual identities for studios, illustrating, building research-driven narratives, teaching students and even tattooing. Currently based in Barcelona, I'm diving deeper into a Master in Design for Emergent Futures at ELISAVA-IAAC. But honestly? I just love building things—whether it's a data visualization, a publication, a classroom experiment, or a story worth telling. I wear different hats—creative director, designer, researcher, teacher—and I've learned that the magic happens when you let them mix. I'm detail-obsessed but never lose sight of the big picture, and I bring the same energy to designing a layout, mentoring a student, or mapping out a concept with a team. have fun looking around and let's build something together!
